A GLANCE AT EMOHR

The Emohr is a frontier and foremost service oriented Human Rights organization in Nigeria, formed   to defend,sustain and promote fundamental Human Right of all person guaranteed in the Nigerian constitution, the Africa charter on the Human Right of all person guaranteed in the universal declaration of Human Rights other informational conventional and convenants on Human Rights to campaign for full implementation of Human Right including cultural, economic and social right and above all, the vision of the organization is to establish a system of prompt and efficient assistance for the needy person that Human Right are violated.


Aims And OBJECTIVES

The aims and objectives of Emohr can be cited on chapter one section 2 subsection 1 to 7 of the 2005 Emohr shall continue to be as follows;
(1) To defend, sustain and promote fundamental Human Right guaranteed in the Nigerian constitution, the Africa charter on Human Rights and other international conventions and convenants on Human Rights.
(2) To campaign for full implementation of Human Rights including cultural economic and social rights
(3) To advocate for the ratification and implementation of all international conventions and obligation almost at promoting Human Rights in Nigeria
(4) To establish a system of prompt and efficient assistance for the needy person whose rights are violated
(5) To ally with other organization committed to the ideas of a Nigeria society founded on particularly democracy and social justice
(6) To campaign for, promote and support legislations and other activities that are geared towards the improvement of human rights in Nigeria
(7) To create and support research centers and programmes for the study, teaching and monitoring of Human Rights in Nigeria
(8) To sustain close in links and collaborate with other organizations with similar aims and objectives anywhere in the world.
